About AutomotoHR

Simplifying payroll, HR, management, benefits, and compliance for everyone.

A company's success hinges on employee satisfaction. When employees have positive experiences, it fuels growth, retains customers, and keeps staff engaged.

With this in mind, we started AutomotoHR in 2011. Our goal was to set your staff free to do exceptional work, and that remains our driving force to this day. We give HR experts, employees, and organizations the tools to simplify complex tasks and save time. This lets them concentrate on helping their teams, doing well in their jobs, and growing their businesses.

An Award-Winning Solution to Achieve People Operations Excellence

In 2018, AutomotoHR achieved a significant feat by winning the AWA Award. This achievement not only underscores the innovation and dedication of the AutomotoHR team but also highlights the real-world impact of their solution. By receiving this prestigious award, AutomotoHR stands as a testament to its commitment to excellence and its ability to provide businesses with an invaluable tool to navigate the complex landscape of management, payroll, HR, and compliance. This accomplishment serves as a shining milestone in AutomotoHR's journey, showcasing its commitment to simplifying operations and empowering organizations to thrive.
